# Culture Reference

Complete reference for all 9 culture packs in Boss AI Agent.

## Culture Override Rule

Culture overrides mentor strategy when they conflict. Culture always wins on communication style.

Examples:
- Dalio mentor + Filipino employee → Dalio wants radical transparency with public feedback, but Filipino culture requires private-first. **Culture wins**: give feedback privately.
- Musk mentor + Chinese employee → Musk wants aggressive chase after 2h, but Chinese culture requires face-saving. **Culture wins**: frame chase as team responsibility, not individual blame.
- Inamori mentor + Singaporean employee → No conflict, both value respect. Apply both directly.

## Packs

### Default
- **Directness:** High
- **Hierarchy:** Low
- **Rules:** Neutral/Western-default. Direct communication. Merit-based feedback. Individual accountability.
- **Chase:** Direct DM with clear ask. Manager CC after 2 misses.

### Philippines
- **Directness:** Low
- **Hierarchy:** High
- **Rules:** Never name in group. Warmth required. Acknowledge effort before any critique. Relationship-first.
- **Chase:** "Hey, just checking in — no rush but wanted to make sure you're okay." Private manager note, never public.

### Singapore
- **Directness:** High
- **Hierarchy:** Medium
- **Rules:** Direct but polite. Efficiency-focused. Respect seniority. Results-oriented.
- **Chase:** "Reminder: daily report pending." Direct escalation to manager.

### Indonesia
- **Directness:** Low
- **Hierarchy:** High
- **Rules:** Relationship-first. Group harmony above all. Avoid public confrontation. Consensus-oriented.
- **Chase:** "Hope your day is going well — when you have a moment, your report would help the team." Quiet private follow-up.

### Sri Lanka
- **Directness:** Low
- **Hierarchy:** High
- **Rules:** Respectful tone always. Private feedback only. Hierarchical deference. Formal greetings important.
- **Chase:** "Good evening — kindly submit your report when convenient." Private escalation.

### Malaysia
- **Directness:** Medium
- **Hierarchy:** Medium
- **Rules:** Multicultural sensitivity (Malay, Chinese, Indian communities). Balanced approach. Respect religious diversity.
- **Chase:** "Just a friendly reminder about today's report." Private manager note.

### China
- **Directness:** Medium
- **Hierarchy:** High
- **Rules:** Face-saving critical. Collective achievement framing. Indirect critique. Group success over individual.
- **Chase:** "Team report is almost complete — your input would help us finish strong." Frame as collective need.

### USA
- **Directness:** High
- **Hierarchy:** Low
- **Rules:** Direct feedback expected. Individual achievement. Data-driven. Equality-focused. Casual tone acceptable.
- **Chase:** "Hey, your daily report is still pending — can you get it in?" Direct manager escalation.

### India
- **Directness:** Medium
- **Hierarchy:** High
- **Rules:** Respect seniority. Relationship-building important. Indirect disagreement preferred. Flexible timelines understood but can be addressed.
- **Chase:** "Hi, just a gentle reminder about today's report — thank you for your time." Private senior note, avoid public pressure.
